Trini Style Chicken

DESCRIPTIONS

this spicy Trini Style Chicken with bok choy receives rave reviews from our reviewers.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 packet sazon seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons white sufar
  • 3 bok choy stalks, chopped

STEPS

  1. Stir together the garlic powder, salt, cayenne pepper, and sazon seasoning in a shallow dish.
  2. Heat a Dutch oven or heavy skillet with a lid over medium heat for at least 3 minutes
  3. Remove cover, and cook for another 15 to 20 minutes, letting the juices cook down until they are almost gone.
